Summary of Benefits and Coverage (SBC) — Sample
Health Plan
Plan Overview
This is a synthetic Summary of Benefits and Coverage document created for educational purposes
only. It does not represent a real insurance plan or any real individual's coverage.
Monthly Premium
The monthly premium for this Gold PPO plan is $500. Premiums are subject to change annually based
on plan renewal terms.
Annual Deductible
The annual deductible for this plan is $2,000 per individual. This is the amount a member must pay out
of pocket before the plan begins covering most services.
Copay Structure
After the deductible is met, the member is responsible for a 10% copayment on most covered services.
Preventive care visits are covered at 100% with no copay required.
Out-of-Pocket Maximum
The out-of-pocket maximum for this plan is $6,000 per individual per plan year. Once this limit is
reached, the plan covers 100% of additional covered services for the remainder of the year.
Network Tier
This plan uses the Gold network tier, which includes access to a broad network of primary care
physicians, specialists, and hospitals.
Common Medical Events
If you have an outpatient surgery: you may pay a $250 copay per visit for a physician/surgeon and 10%
coinsurance for the facility fee. If you need mental health, behavioral health, or substance abuse
services: you may pay a $30 copay per office visit.
Prescription Drug Coverage
Generic drugs: $10 copay. Preferred brand drugs: $35 copay. Non-preferred brand drugs: $60 copay.
Specialty drugs: 20% coinsurance.
